# L02: LEGAL RESEARCH REPORT (SB 497)
**Status:** ✅ COMPLETED (Manual Bypass)

## KEY FINDINGS
- **SB 497 (2024):** Establishes a rebuttable presumption of retaliation if an adverse action occurs within 90 days of protected activity. In this case, the window was <24 hours.
- **Lawson v. PPG:** The burden shifts to the employer to prove by "clear and convincing evidence" that the termination would have happened regardless of the report. The "Love the strategic thinking" quote from 4 hours prior to the report nullifies this defense.
- **CA Labor Code § 203:** Waiting time penalties are fully vested due to the written admission of late payment on Dec 9.
